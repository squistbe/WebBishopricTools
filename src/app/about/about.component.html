LDS Bishopric Tools is free service created by a Ward Clerk that felt the pain of what LDS Tools is lacking for Bishoprics. Some of the features include:
  • Tracking Sacrament Meeting attendance.
  • Sacrament Meeting Prayer tracker with the option of having the tool choose for you!
  • Sacrament Meeting Calendar that tracks songs, prayers, speakers and musical numbers.
  • Automatically track action items for Bishopric members such as annual interviews or other assignments.
  • And most importantly, track calling and their status. I found this the most challenging part of staying organized as a Bishopric. Did we sustain them? Did we pray about it? Did they get set apart? Did we call for an interview? Did we remember to release them?
